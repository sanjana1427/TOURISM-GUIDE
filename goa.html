It is the smallest states in India in terms of geographical area. Yet Goa carries a vast spectrum of cultural practices. Portuguese, Maharashtrian and Konkani influences are evident in language, food, architecture, dress and dance. Numerous festivals are celebrated here that show affirmation to its multicultural glory. Goa is a blend of ancient Portuguese colony, sandy shores, forests, monuments and delightful cuisine- you can't get enough of Goa.
