❣️A dream holiday destination for the entire family, Visakhapatnam is more than just an Industrial city. Situated in the east coast of Andhra Pradesh, Visakhapatnam is named after Visakha, the Hindu God of valour and is more popularly known as Vizag. Apart from being known for the numerous industries, having the country’s oldest shipyards and being the headquarters of the Eastern Naval Command of the Indian Navy; Vizag is also known as a major tourist destination with hordes of tourists thronging this beautiful city of Andhra Pradesh every year. With sprawling attractive beaches, ancient temples, beautiful caves and splendid valleys, this city of Destiny has something to offer for everyone.
